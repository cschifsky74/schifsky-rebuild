## Maintain a Thriving Lawn in Every Season

A healthy lawn in Minneapolis depends on year-round maintenance that adapts to changing weather. From spring growth to winter prep, your grass needs proper attention to thrive. Regular mowing, fertilization, and seasonal treatments prevent weed infestations, patchy areas, and pest damage. Working with a lawn care team like Schifsky Companies gives you access to expert timing and techniques that boost soil health and help grass roots grow deeper and stronger. Our professionals evaluate the needs of your lawn and apply solutions that match the soil, sun exposure, and type of grass to maintain a vibrant appearance and prevent common lawn issues.

### Consistent Mowing and Clean Edges

Mowing is more than a visual task. Cutting grass at the right height encourages thicker growth and reduces the risk of lawn stress, especially in warm summer months. Keeping blades sharp ensures a clean cut that promotes healing and prevents damage to the grass tips. Trimming edges along sidewalks, driveways, and garden beds helps maintain a polished, uniform appearance and prevents weeds from encroaching into the lawn. At Schifsky Companies, our lawn care crew pays close attention to detail, cutting at the right intervals for the season and leaving your lawn looking even and professionally maintained.

### Strategic Fertilization and Weed Prevention

Fertilization gives your lawn the nutrients it needs to grow thick and healthy. Our team applies fertilizer at the right times of year to match your grass type and local conditions. Weeds compete with grass for nutrients and water, so controlling them early in the season is key to preventing widespread growth. Schifsky Companies uses targeted weed control strategies that are effective without damaging surrounding plants. These methods reduce the spread of invasive species and create a stronger lawn that resists future problems. The result is a greener, more robust yard with fewer interruptions in growth.

### Aeration and Irrigation Efficiency

Aeration allows air, water, and nutrients to reach the roots of your grass more effectively, especially if the soil has become compacted from foot traffic or weather conditions. Our aeration services loosen the soil and promote healthy root development. For watering, consistency is key. A well-planned irrigation system distributes the right amount of water across your yard, helping your lawn remain hydrated without waste. Schifsky Companies designs and maintains irrigation systems that support lush grass while adapting to seasonal rainfall and temperature shifts, helping you conserve resources while keeping your lawn green.

### Seasonal Treatments and Lawn Recovery

Each season in Minneapolis brings its own lawn care challenges. In spring, we prepare the lawn with cleanup, pre-emergent weed control, and fertilization. During summer, we monitor moisture levels and apply mid-season treatments to combat pests and nutrient loss. In fall, we focus on strengthening the grass roots with feeding and aeration to prepare for winter dormancy. Before the snow falls, our team ensures the lawn is clean and trimmed to avoid mold or snow damage. These seasonal steps protect your investment and help your lawn return stronger each year.
