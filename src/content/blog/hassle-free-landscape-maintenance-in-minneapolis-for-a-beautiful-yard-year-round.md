## Mulched Beds and Pruning for Plant Health

Clean beds protect plant roots and create visual contrast. We refresh mulch annually to suppress weeds, retain moisture, and insulate seasonal shifts. Our pruning team shapes shrubs, renews flowering branches, and removes deadwood to promote new growth. Clear beds and properly trimmed plants strengthen garden structure and reduce disease risk.

## Spring and Fall Cleanups for Seasonal Transitions

Preparing for cold weather is as important as spring prep. In spring, we clear debris, clean flowerbeds, and rebuild mulch. When autumn arrives, we trim perennials, remove spent foliage, and guard young trees. Timely cleanup prevents disease spread and readies your yard for snow, ensuring healthier rebirth in spring. Proper fall preparation also reduces the risk of pests taking shelter in your yard during the winter months.

## Irrigation Management That Saves Water

Efficient watering keeps plants thriving while lowering bills. We inspect irrigation systems, adjust heads for coverage, and reset schedules based on seasonal rainfall. System testing ensures no leaks or underperforming zones earlier than needed. Smart irrigation keeps moisture consistent and supports sustainable landscape practices. Regular adjustments also help minimize water waste, contributing to environmental conservation.

## Snow Removal and Winter Yard Protection

Snow and ice can damage landscapes if left unchecked. Our winter care clears driveways, walkways, and patios promptly to prevent ice buildup. We apply safe deicing treatments to protect plants and hardscapes. Early snow removal also prevents damage from freeze-thaw cycles, keeping your yard functional during winter.
